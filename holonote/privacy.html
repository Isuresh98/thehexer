Privacy Policy — Holo Note

Holo Note respects your privacy. This app is designed to work offline and keep your notes on your device.

Data Collection

Holo Note does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

How Your Notes Are Stored

Your notes are stored locally on your device using on-device storage (SQLite). No cloud sync is required.

Internet Usage

Holo Note works fully offline. The app does not require internet access to function.

Accounts / Login

Holo Note does not require registration or login.

Backups (Export / Import)

  • Backup files are created only when you choose to export.
  • You control where the backup is saved and how it is shared.
  • The app does not upload backups to any server.

Third-Party Services

Holo Note does not use ads, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

Children’s Privacy

Holo Note does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will update the “Last updated” date on this page.

Contact
