HOLO NOTE

Holo Note is a beautiful offline notes app with folders, search, pin/star and backup/restore. No login. No tracking. Your notes stay on your device.

Key Features

  • Offline notes (no account required)
  • Folders, tags & fast search
  • Pin important notes & star favorites
  • Backup export / import (you control sharing)
  • Modern glass-style dark UI
